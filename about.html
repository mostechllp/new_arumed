About New Arumed

Our Heritage

New Arumed (Pvt) Ltd was established in 2004 as an associate company of the renowned Arumed Group. Formed by a team of healthcare trade experts, our mission has always been to provide unparalleled service to healthcare professionals across Sri Lanka.

With over two decades of experience, we have grown into a trusted name in the pharmaceutical industry, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach.

Our key strategy — concentric diversification — allows us to expand our expertise methodically, offering a wider spectrum of medical products while maintaining deep specialization in each category.

Key Customers

State Pharmaceuticals Corporation (SPC)

SPC is a semi-government organization, functioning as a sole agent for procuring healthcare requirements for the Ministry of Health, Sri Lanka. Procurement is done through global and local tenders.

Criteria for Supplier Selection:

  • Product registration in CDDA
  • Price evaluation based on quality
  • Bid and performance bonds

Medical Supplies Division (MSD)

MSD supports the government's free healthcare policy by gathering and estimating hospital requirements for tenders, making specifications, evaluating samples, and distributing stocked healthcare products to institutions across Sri Lanka.

Cosmetic Drugs and Devices Authority

Regulatory Body of the Ministry of Health, Sri Lanka

CDDA is the regulatory body of the Ministry of Health, where all healthcare products must be registered prior to importing into Sri Lanka. Product registration can be carried out by an authorized marketing agent of any manufacturer, exporter, or local producer.

Required Documentation for Registration:

  • Documents and samples
  • Product sample with original packing, label, and manuals
  • Free sale certificate
  • Authorization letter
  • List of countries indicating the particular product exported and registered
  • Manufacturing country local health authority approval
